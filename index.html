ABOUT ME

Greetings, and thanks for visiting my website portfolio! My name is Shane Falero. I am a recently retired veterinarian with the goal of growing and developing a new career within the software development industry.

After three years of working within the veterinary profession, I opted for a change of career. I have developed excellent customer service skills, superlative communication skills, and have developed a bit of a creative spark as well. Software Development gives me the opportunity to build something of my own, problem solve, and work collaboratively on projects with others while utilizing some of the necessary skills I have acquired along the way. I wanted a change in pace to allow myself a bit more freedom on the home-front and be able to manage my time more efficiently. I look forward to what I can achieve within the field!
